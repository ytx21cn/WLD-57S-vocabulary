marvel n.

Intense surprise or interest; astonishment.

misty adj.

Vague; confused.

porch n.

A covered area adjoining an entrance to a building and usually having a separate roof.

quiver v.

To shake or move with a slight trembling motion.

repentance n.

A feeling of regret or contrition.

vainly adv.

Unsuccessfully; ineffectually.
